Benchmark Logic

How alignment shapes the output.

Alignment Score Calculation

Each of the three inputs is checked against its target band. Alignment % = the fraction of inputs inside their optimal band × 100.

alignment = (inputs_in_band / 3) × 100
All 3 in band100% aligned
2 of 3 in band67% aligned
1 of 3 in band33% aligned
None in band0% aligned

How Alignment Affects Confidence

Alignment feeds directly into the reliability band formula alongside classifier confidence probability.

reliability = (classifier_prob × 0.6) + (alignment × 0.4)

A student with high study hours but low attendance may still achieve a good score prediction — but with lower reliability because the behavior pattern sits further from the learned high-confidence zone.

Actionable Guidance

Three levers. Measurable impact.

01

Nudge Study Hours

If weekly hours are below 16, each additional focused hour produces measurable score lift on the trend curve — up to the 20–24h plateau. The trend endpoint shows exactly where the curve starts tapering.

Target: 16–24 h/wk
02

Boost Attendance

Attendance is the fastest lever to improve confidence reliability. Even moving from 78% to 86% can shift the benchmark alignment from 33% to 67%, materially changing the reliability band outcome.

Target: ≥ 85%
03

Raise Participation

Participation below 6/10 marks a boundary where the classifier begins to route profiles toward lower productivity bands. Crossing 6.0 stabilizes the classification outcome for most attendance levels.

Target: 6.0 – 8.5 / 10
Score Bands

Three performance trajectories.

85 +
Elite Trajectory

Operating at or near all three optimal input bands. Model indicates consistently strong learning and output efficiency. Score lift to this tier typically requires 85%+ attendance, 20+ study hours, and participation above 7.

70 – 84
Competitive Trajectory

Strong baseline with room for compounding gains. Usually 1–2 inputs near the optimal band. Small adjustments in the weakest dimension (attendance or study hours) typically produce the largest score lift.

< 70
Recovery Trajectory

Profile sits outside the strongest scoring band. Multiple inputs below their optimal zone. The console's suggestions target the highest-leverage intervention — the lever that produces the most immediate alignment gain.
