Ingest

Behavior vectors enter the pipeline

Weekly self-study hours, attendance percentage, and class participation are treated as the three-dimensional behavior spine. Raw inputs are normalized via a feature scaler trained on 1M+ student records.

Interpret

Three models create a composite reading

A gradient-boosted regressor scores academic output. A classifier maps productivity to High / Medium / Low bands. A K-means cluster assigns a behavioral archetype. All three run simultaneously — not sequentially.

Every result is measured against an empirically-derived optimal band: 16–24 study hours, 85–95% attendance, 6–8.5 participation. Alignment % directly shapes confidence reliability.
